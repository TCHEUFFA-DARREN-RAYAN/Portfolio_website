About Me

Passionate embedded systems and IoT developer with expertise in creating innovative, connected solutions that bridge the physical and digital worlds. I specialize in designing and developing intelligent devices that collect, process, and transmit data to create meaningful user experiences.

My journey in technology began with a fascination for how everyday objects could become "smart" through the integration of sensors, microcontrollers, and connectivity. Over the years, I've worked on projects ranging from industrial automation systems to consumer IoT devices, always focusing on reliability, efficiency, and user-centric design.

I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives. Whether it's optimizing energy consumption in smart homes or developing robust communication protocols for industrial applications, I'm driven by the challenge of making complex systems work seamlessly.

Fun Fact

When I'm not coding or debugging hardware, you can find me building custom mechanical keyboards or experimenting with home automation projects that sometimes confuse my family members!
