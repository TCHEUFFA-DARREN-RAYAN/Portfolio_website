About Me

Hi, I'm Darren Rayan Tcheuffa — a passionate embedded and full-stack developer with expertise in IoT and full-stack web development. I create innovative, connected solutions that bridge the physical and digital worlds, designing intelligent devices and applications that collect, process, and transmit data to deliver meaningful user experiences.
